Privacy Policy

Last updated 18:44 EST November 14 2019

What information do we collect?

We ask for some information used to identify you as a user when joining Musicavis. This includes your username, password, and email address. No other information is collected from you. It is advised not to use your real name as your username. You can log in with either your username or email address.

We also collect statistical data based on our users' practice sessions. This information is aggregate. It is not associated with you or your account.

What do you do with this information?

Musicavis never outsources your personal information to third parties for advertising or promotional purposes. We may need to give your contact information to a third party if we are unable to collect the money you owe to Musicavis.

We will occasionally use your email address to send you information regarding Musicavis if you consent. These include information related to practicing, product features, and promotions and discounts.. All of your data is stored in our database.

The information we collect on our users' practices is used for analytical purposes to improve our platform and to improve your user experience. We do not provide such information to our advertisers.

Is my information secure?

We are confident in our ability to keep your information safe. We have taken steps to ensure our databases are secure.

Your personal information is protected by your password. It is encrypted in our databases to ensure maximum security. Never share this password with anyone. Musicavis will never contact you unsolicited to ask for your password. Please contact us immediately if this event happens.

What about cookies?

Musicavis stores a session cookie on your computer to track you while you are on the site. It expires once you close the website. This cookie contains four strings ("words") that are only meaningful to our servers.

Our platform also stores a cookie on your computer when you accept our GDPR notice regarding cookies. It expires 1 (one) month after its creation. This cookie contains no personal data. It is only used to know whether you accepted the notice.

The information stored in our cookies can not be used by third parties to gain access to your account information.

Do you have any question?

We take your privacy very seriously. We realize everyone has a different view of privacy. If you have concerns, or if any aspect of our privacy policy is unclear to you, please contact us. We will be happy to discuss the matter with you.
